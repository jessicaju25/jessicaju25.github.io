Blog on Food Donation App

  • by:Jessica
  • date:June 20, 2023
To view the code, click on the image or "Blog on Food Donation App" title!

I received the prestigious Innovation Award from the Ansan civic group for the app's impactful contribution in addressing food waste and fostering community engagement.

The Food Donation app is a sophisticated mobile application designed to streamline the process of food donation. Built for Android platform, the app combines elegant design and advanced technical features to provide a seamless user experience.

Through seamless integration with a Food Bank website, the app displays relevant and dynamic content directly within its interface. It leverages WebView and advanced JavaScript techniques to extract specific information and eliminate distractions like menu bars and footers. This integration ensures that users can focus on essential details without being overwhelmed by unnecessary elements. The app drove a 3% increase in local food donations and fostered community engagement.
