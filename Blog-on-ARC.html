Welcome to the revolutionary "Accessible Reading Companion" chrome extension, where web accessibility meets cutting-edge technology. As the developer, my vision is to empower every user with seamless access to online content, regardless of their abilities.

Picture a chrome extension that effortlessly converts digital text into clear, engaging speech, providing you with a supportive reading companion at your fingertips. Our user-friendly interface offers a concise summary of web content, allowing you to choose whether to hear the full content with a simple keystroke.

We are dedicated to adhering to the highest accessibility standards, ensuring equal access to information for all users. The "Accessible Reading Companion" is your gateway to an inclusive digital world, where every individual can thrive.
